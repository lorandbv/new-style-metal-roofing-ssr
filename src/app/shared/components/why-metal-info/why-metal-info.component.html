Why Metal Roofing?

Lifespan

A metal tile roof, if assembled as recommended by the specialists, must have the same lifetime as the construction that it was mounted on, at least 50-60 years. The metal roofing is resistant to rain, wind, snow, mold, so that the metal roof tile will not be affected.

Weight

The strength and the light weight of metal tiles make them the favorite competitor for the title of best covering. Metal tiles are lightweight compared to shingles.

Tightness

The metal roofing modules grip system by overlap and securing with self-tapping screws ensure the perfect sealing and prevents water leaks under the covering.

Fire Resistance

High degree of resistance to the propagation and spread of fire.

Aesthetics

The roof should highlight the beauty of the building. The metal tile roofing is the only solution that offers a wide range of colors, materials and forms, so it adapts and fits any type of construction and integrates with the style of the community to which belongs.
